# Privacy Policy

**Cooper IT Consulting**
*Last updated: February 12, 2026*

## Overview

Cooper IT Consulting ("Company", "we", "us") operates the Hal application ("App"), which integrates with QuickBooks Online to provide accounting and financial management services.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you connect your QuickBooks Online account to our App.

## Information We Collect

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, the App may access:
- **Company information** (company name, address, fiscal year)
- **Financial data** (invoices, customers, vendors, payments, estimates)
- **Reports** (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)

We do not collect personal information beyond what is available through the QuickBooks Online API as authorized by you.

## How We Use Your Information

Your QuickBooks Online data is used solely to:
- Display financial summaries and reports upon your request
- Respond to your queries about invoices, customers, vendors, and other accounting data
- Generate briefings and notifications as configured by you

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is processed on privately-owned, self-hosted infrastructure (not cloud-hosted third-party services).
- Data in transit is encrypted via TLS.
- Data at rest is stored in encrypted databases with access restricted to authorized personnel only.
- No QuickBooks Online data is shared with third parties.

## Data Retention

- QuickBooks Online data is accessed on-demand and is not permanently cached beyond what is necessary for active operations.
- Token credentials are stored securely with file-level encryption and restricted permissions.

## Your Rights

You may at any time:
- **Disconnect** the App from your QuickBooks Online account via the Intuit App Center
- **Request deletion** of any cached data by contacting us
- **Review** what data the App has accessed through QuickBooks Online audit logs

## Third-Party Services

The App connects to QuickBooks Online via Intuit's official OAuth 2.0 API. Your use of QuickBooks Online is governed by [Intuit's Privacy Statement](https://www.intuit.com/privacy/statement/).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
